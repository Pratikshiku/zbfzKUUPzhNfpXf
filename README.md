### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (any of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Functional Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Parking Space Information
5. Community Information
6. Nearby Services
7. Questionnaire Survey
8. Caring for the Elderly
9. Notices and Announcements
10. Message Feedback
11. Personal Center
12. My Favorites
13. Customer Service Center


**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Customer Management
4. Online Repair Management
5. Complaint and Suggestion Management
6. Parking Space Information Management
7. Parking Space Rental and Purchase Management
8. Community Information Management
9. Nearby Services Management
10. Questionnaire Survey Management
11. Survey Participation Management
12. Caring for the Elderly Management
13. Message Board Management
14. System Management (Customer Service Center, Carousel Management, Notices and Announcements)
